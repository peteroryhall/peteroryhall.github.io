I am a dual PhD student at the University of Oxford (Department of Physics & Department of Biochemistry, Tucker Lab) and Scripps Research (Ward & Forli Labs), working at the intersection of computational and structural biology. My research focuses on structure-guided therapeutic design targeting membrane proteins, combining experimental structural approaches with computational methods to understand how these proteins can be selectively modulated in disease.

Outside the lab I enjoy marathon running, writing, and travelling.
