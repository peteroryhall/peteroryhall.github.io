Research

At the University of Oxford, I work across Physics and Biochemistry in the Tucker Lab, using cryo-EM and biophysical approaches to study potassium ion channels.

At Scripps Research, I work across the Ward, Forli, and Parker Labs, applying computational biophysics and structure-guided methods to build a mechanism-to-therapeutics pipeline for solute transporters.
